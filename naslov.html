Why Fundamentals and News Are Important in Trading?

In trading, fundamentals and news play a crucial role in understanding the intrinsic value of an asset and predicting its future price movements. Fundamentals refer to the underlying factors that affect the financial health and performance of a company, sector, or economy, such as earnings reports, interest rates, GDP growth, and employment data. News, on the other hand, includes real-time events like geopolitical developments, central bank announcements, and unexpected market-moving events. Together, they provide traders with a comprehensive view of the market, helping them make informed decisions. For example, a strong earnings report can boost a company's stock price, while a sudden interest rate hike by a central bank can cause currency fluctuations. By incorporating fundamentals and news into their strategy, traders can identify opportunities, manage risks, and stay ahead of market trends.

Combining FUNDAMENTAL analysis with TECHNICAL analysis is a powerful approach to trading, as it allows you to understand both the "why" and the "when" of market movements.
